# Rocio Competitive Benchmark

Date: 2026-07-05

## Benchmark Decision

Primary benchmark: Planta.

Reason: Rocio's commercial path is recurring plant care, reminders, trust, and habit formation. PictureThis and Plantum are useful references for scanner positioning, ASO, and paywall pressure, but Rocio v1 should not compete by claiming bigger AI.

## Competitive Matrix

| Area | Planta | PictureThis | Plantum | Rocio v1 target |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Core job | Ongoing plant care | Identify plants fast | AI diagnosis and plant care | Care for flowers in Spanish |
| Revenue signal | Annual subscription | Subscription / premium ID | IAP premium tiers | Free v1, StoreKit v1.1 |
| Onboarding | Strong habit setup | Scanner-first | AI-first | 3-step native onboarding |
| Scanner claims | Secondary | Aggressive identification | AI plant doctor | Honest local candidates |
| Privacy angle | Not primary | Not primary | Not primary | Local-first, no tracking v1 |
| Language angle | Broad/localized | Broad/localized | Broad/localized | Native English/Spanish with Mexico/LatAm flower context |
| Differentiation | Full care platform | Massive database | AI diagnosis | Simple private flower companion |

## Pricing Strategy

Do not add monetization before v1 App Review.

Recommended v1.1 pricing after acceptance:

- Rocio Pro one-time unlock: US$14.99.
- Alternative annual plan: US$19.99/year only if v1.1 adds recurring value such as seasonal packs, weather-aware care, or expanded catalog.
- No external payment links in the app.
- No scanner accuracy paywall until the scanner is provider-backed and review-safe.

## Product Implications

- Build retention around saved garden, watering cadence, calendar, reminders, and App Intents.
- Use scanner as an assistive entry point, not the main promise.
- Keep App Store copy conservative: "candidatos", "experimental", "verifica la ficha".
- Make screenshots show daily care and privacy, not just flowers.

## Open Checks Before Submission
