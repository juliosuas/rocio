Support center

Help for the iOS 1.0 feature candidate (build 1) and its plant scanner.

Current availability: Rocío is not yet published through TestFlight or the App Store. The web demo is a separate preview that stores data only in your browser.

Quick fixes

My plants do not appear.

Make sure you signed in with the same account and have a connection so synchronization can finish. Recent changes remain cached and will be retried.

The recovery link expired or does not open.

Request a new email from the app and open only the latest link on the same iPhone. Do not share the link or any code.

I do not receive reminders.

Enable the reminder from the plant and confirm that iOS allows notifications for Rocío. The app does not request permission until you take that action.

The scanner is wrong.

Use natural light, frame one plant clearly, include leaves, flowers, or fruit when possible, and review the candidates before changing care. Plant.id and the local flower fallback are assistive, not definitive.

The web demo does not synchronize.

This is expected: the demo does not create accounts or use Supabase. Export your data before clearing browser storage.

Send a request

Report bugs, privacy requests, or access problems through GitHub Issues. Include version 1.0 (build 1), your iPhone model, and iOS version, but do not include email addresses, photos, recovery links, tokens, street addresses, or other personal information.
